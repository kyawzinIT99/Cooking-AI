## Welcome to CulinaryAI

CulinaryAI is your all-in-one cooking assistant website. It covers 9 world cuisines, provides step-by-step recipes with full nutrition info, lets you watch YouTube cooking videos right inside the app, gives you professional kitchen tools, and features a real AI chef powered by OpenAI that answers all your cooking and nutrition questions.

**To open the app:** Simply double-click `index.html` or open it in any modern web browser.

No installation. No account needed. Works offline for most features (except AI chat and videos, which require internet).

## 4. Recipe Library

### Searching for Recipes
- **Search bar** (hero section): Type a dish name, ingredient, or cuisine. Suggestions appear live as you type.
- **Quick tags**: Click the pre-set tags (Butter Chicken, Pad Thai, Phở, etc.) under the search bar for instant results.
- **Filter buttons**: Use the row of filter buttons above the recipe grid (All / Indian / Chinese / Thai / Vietnamese / Myanmar / Bakery / Luxury).

### Recipe Cards
Each card shows:
- **Food photo** (Unsplash) with a "▶ Watch Video" badge (if a video is available)
- **Recipe name** and description
- **Diet tags** (e.g. Vegan, Gluten-Free, High Protein)
- **Time** and **serves** count
- **Calorie badge** — colour coded:
  - 🟢 Green = Light (under 250 kcal)
  - 🟡 Amber = Moderate (250–449 kcal)
  - 🟠 Orange = Rich (450+ kcal)
  - All labelled **"(Estimated)"**
- **Mini nutrition grid**: Protein · Carbs · Fat · Fiber per serving
- Three action buttons:
  - 📖 **Full Recipe** — opens the detailed recipe modal
  - 🎬 **Watch** — opens the YouTube video directly
  - 🤍 / ❤️ **Save** — saves/unsaves the recipe (stored in your browser)

### Load More
Click **"Load More Recipes"** at the bottom of the grid to show additional recipes beyond the first 9.

## 7. Chef AI — OpenAI Chat

The centrepiece of CulinaryAI — a real AI chef powered by **OpenAI GPT-4o-mini**.

### Opening the Chat
- Click the floating **👨‍🍳** button (bottom-right)
- Or click **"Ask Chef AI"** in the AI section
- Or click the demo chat area

### How the AI Works
The AI is **strictly limited to cooking topics only**. It will:
- ✅ Answer recipe questions
- ✅ Explain cooking techniques
- ✅ Give nutrition and calorie information
- ✅ Suggest substitutions for ingredients
- ✅ Help with meal planning
- ✅ Discuss cuisines, spice levels, baking tips
- ❌ Refuse non-cooking topics (math, coding, news, etc.)

### Chat Status Indicator
The coloured dot in the chat header tells you the AI mode:
| Dot | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 Green (pulsing) | OpenAI GPT-4o connected and ready |
| 🟡 Amber (fast pulse) | AI is thinking… |
| ⚪ Grey | Using built-in Chef mode (no API key) |

### Quick Suggestion Buttons
Six pre-filled questions appear below the chat:
- 🥥 Coconut chicken ideas
- 🔥 Calorie check for Butter Chicken
- 🥢 Wok hei secrets
- 🥚 Egg substitutes in baking
- 💪 High-protein low-carb meal ideas
- 🐟 Myanmar Mohinga recipe

Click any of them to send that question instantly.

### Typing Your Own Question
1. Click the text field at the bottom
2. Type your cooking question
3. Press **Enter** or click the **→** send button

### Multi-Turn Conversation
The AI remembers the last 7 turns of conversation so you can ask follow-up questions:
> You: "How do I make Pad Thai?"
> AI: [gives recipe]
> You: "Can I make it vegetarian?"
> AI: [adjusts based on previous answer]

### Managing Your API Key

Click the **⚙️** gear icon in the chat header to open the API settings panel.

**To update or change your key:**
1. Click ⚙️
2. Paste your OpenAI API key (starts with `sk-`)
3. Click **Save**
4. The status dot turns green — you're connected

**To remove the key:**
1. Click ⚙️
2. Click **Clear**
3. The AI switches to built-in keyword mode

> **Note:** Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device.

### Built-in Chef Mode (No Key)
If no API key is stored, the AI uses the built-in knowledge base with keyword matching. It covers:
- Substitutions (butter, eggs, flour, dairy, etc.)
- Techniques (knife skills, wok hei, bread-making, etc.)
- Temperatures (baking, frying, roasting)
- Cuisines (Indian, Chinese, Thai, Vietnamese, Myanmar)
- Ingredients → recipe suggestions
- Calorie-level guidance

## 14. Tips & Tricks

### Getting the Best from Chef AI
- **Be specific:** "How do I make Pad Thai with tofu instead of shrimp?" works better than "Pad Thai?"
- **Ask for calories:** "How many calories in a serving of Butter Chicken?" gives detailed macros
- **Ask follow-ups:** The AI remembers the last 7 turns — you can ask "Can I make that vegan?" after a recipe answer
- **Substitutions:** "What can I use instead of heavy cream in Butter Chicken?"
- **Technique help:** "How do I get wok hei at home without a professional stove?"

### Saving Recipes
- Click the 🤍 heart on any recipe card to save it
- Saved recipes persist in your browser even after closing
- Click ❤️ again to unsave

### Watching and Cooking
- Open a recipe modal → the YouTube video plays at the top
- Read the ingredients list while watching
- Click ▲ Hide to collapse the video and see the full recipe

### Using the Timer While Cooking
1. Open the Tools section in a separate browser tab for the timer
2. Set your cooking preset or custom time
3. Leave it running while you use other parts of the app

## 15. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| Chef AI gives "error" message | Check your internet connection. The AI falls back to built-in mode automatically. |
| "OpenAI unavailable" warning appears | Your API key may have expired or hit its limit. Get a new key from platform.openai.com |
| Photos not loading | Unsplash CDN requires internet. Recipes show an emoji fallback without photos. |
| Videos not playing | YouTube requires internet. Make sure you're connected. |
| Timer not vibrating | Vibration requires a phone/tablet with vibration support. Desktop browsers don't vibrate. |
| Recipe not found | Some luxury/bakery/Myanmar items show "coming soon" — full recipes are being added. |
| Chat doesn't open | Click the floating 👨‍🍳 button or the "Ask Chef AI" button in the AI section. |
| Saved recipes disappeared | localStorage was cleared (browser data cleared). Recipes are stored locally per browser. |

### System Requirements
- Any modern browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- Internet connection for: AI chat, YouTube videos, recipe photos
- No internet needed for: Recipe data, all 5 kitchen tools, technique library, dark mode, animations
